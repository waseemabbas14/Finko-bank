Common Questions

What is the minimum loan amount?
Minimums vary across lenders like Think Tank and La Trobe Financial, typically beginning at $150,000 to $200,000 depending on your fund's financial position and property value.
How do SMSF loan rates compare?
SMSF loans often carry higher rates than standard residential loans, though Finco Capital accesses competitive rates through our lenders. For example, rates start from around 6.64% p.a.* depending on LVR and loan structure.
What properties can I buy?
SMSF loans cover residential investment properties—houses, units, townhouses—and commercial properties satisfying the sole purpose test, including specialised SMSF commercial loans.
What are typical LVRs and loan terms?
Residential LVRs generally range from 70-80%, meaning your fund provides a 20-30% deposit. Terms extend up to 30 years, with both Principal & Interest and Interest-Only options.
How long does the process take?
Due to compliance requirements, SMSF loans usually take 4 to 8 weeks from application to settlement. Our streamlined approach aims to minimise delays through expert documentation handling.
