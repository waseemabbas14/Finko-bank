Common Questions

What is the minimum loan amount?
Typically starts at $100,000, varying by lender.
How do Commercial SMSF loan rates compare?
Rates differ from residential loans and depend on lender assessments and LVR. We secure competitive financing tailored to your SMSF's needs.
What properties qualify for Commercial SMSF loans?
Commercial properties satisfying the 'sole purpose test' for retirement benefits—offices, retail spaces, warehouses, industrial units—and those occupied by related businesses.
What are typical LVRs and loan terms?
LVRs generally up to 80%, subject to property type. Loan terms are aligned with commercial lease durations and your fund's investment strategy.
What fees apply?
Standard government and valuation fees apply, alongside:

• Application Fee (from $795)
• SMSF Review Fee ($695 for existing funds)
• Establishment Fee (from 1.00% of loan amount)5

Discuss all fees with your adviser for transparency.
How long does the process take?
Due to SMSF and commercial property complexities, expect 4 to 8 weeks from application to settlement; our expertise streamlines this timeframe.
