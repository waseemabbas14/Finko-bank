Frequently Asked

What does borrowing power mean?
Borrowing power is an estimate of how much a lender might be willing to lend you based on your income, expenses and existing commitments. It is not a guarantee of approval.
Is this the exact amount I will be approved for?
No. This is an indicative estimate. Final approval depends on lender policies, supporting documents and credit checks.
