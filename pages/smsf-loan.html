SMSF Loans – Invest Through Your Super

Self-Managed Superannuation Fund (SMSF) loans enable trustees to invest in residential or commercial property while leveraging the tax benefits of superannuation. Finco Capital connects you with specialist SMSF lenders offering competitive rates and expert guidance.

SMSF Loan Types

  • SMSF Residential Property Loans: Purchase rental properties within your fund
  • SMSF Commercial Property Loans: Acquire commercial properties for investment
  • SMSF Refinancing: Restructure existing SMSF loans

Why SMSF Loans Make Sense

SMSF loans allow you to build wealth within your superannuation structure while maintaining control over investments. Tax benefits and long-term growth potential make them an attractive strategy for property investment.

Key SMSF Loan Benefits
  • Build property assets within your superannuation
  • Leverage tax-deferred growth and capital gains
  • Maintain full control over investment decisions
  • Access competitive rates from specialist lenders

Frequently Asked Questions

What are SMSF lending restrictions?
SMSF lending must comply with Super law—including the sole purpose test and in-house asset rules. Lenders ensure all loans meet these requirements. We guide you through compliance.
Can I borrow against existing SMSF assets?
Yes. SMSFs with established property or assets can refinance or draw equity to invest further. Specialist lenders understand these structures and can offer flexible solutions.
What documentation do I need?
SMSF lenders require trust deed, financial statements, member details, and fund investment strategy. We'll guide you on collecting the right documentation for lender assessment.
