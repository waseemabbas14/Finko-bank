Frequently Asked Questions

What government assistance is available?
First home buyers can access programs including state-based First Home Owner Grants and the federal Home Guarantee Scheme, allowing eligible buyers to purchase with deposits as low as 5% without paying Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI). Stamp duty concessions also apply.
How much deposit do I really need?
While 20% is ideal to avoid LMI, many schemes facilitate purchases with as little as 5%. We tailor strategies based on your finances and lender policies.
What interest rates can I expect?
Rates are competitive and depend on loan type, deposit, and financial situation. We negotiate on your behalf to secure the best rate available.
How long does the process take?
Pre-approval typically takes 3-5 business days after document submission, with full approval in 2-4 weeks once a property is found.
What's the difference between variable and fixed rates?
Variable rates fluctuate with the market providing flexibility; fixed rates lock repayments for set terms offering certainty. We help you choose what fits best.
What is Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI)?
LMI is a premium protecting lenders when deposits are under 20%. We explain costs and help minimise or avoid it where possible.
