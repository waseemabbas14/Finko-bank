Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical lending timeline?
Most commercial loans are approved within 5-10 business days, depending on complexity and documentation completeness. Fast-track options are available for simple transactions.
Do I need security for a commercial loan?
Many commercial loans require security (property, equipment, or business assets), though unsecured options are available for smaller amounts or well-established businesses with strong financials.
Can startups get commercial loans?
Yes, though startups typically need to demonstrate a viable business plan, personal guarantees, and security. Speak with us about options tailored to new businesses.
