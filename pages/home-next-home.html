Buy My Next Home

Planning to buy your next home? This guide helps you understand options for selling & buying, bridging finance, and timing your move to minimise risk.

Key considerations

Decide whether to sell first or buy first, assess your deposit and borrowing power, and consider temporary bridging finance if you need to buy before selling.

Typical steps

  • Calculate your borrowing power for the new purchase.
  • Discuss selling strategy and timing with your agent.
  • Evaluate bridging loan options if you need to buy before selling.
  • Plan for settlement dates and overlap of financing.
