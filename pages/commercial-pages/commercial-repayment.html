Common Questions

What interest rates can I expect?
Rates vary depending on lender, property type, and your financial profile. Our panel offers highly competitive fixed and variable rates. We’ll secure the best rate for your circumstances.
How much can I borrow for commercial property?
Borrowing capacity is primarily calculated based on the property’s value and income generation. Most lenders offer LVRs up to 80%, with specialist lenders sometimes exceeding this. Loan sizes can reach $25 million.
Can I use my SMSF to purchase commercial property?
Yes. SMSF commercial property loans are a powerful strategy for investment growth. We facilitate specialised SMSF loans with competitive rates and guide you through compliance and documentation specifics.
What is the typical approval timeline?
Commercial loan approvals require thorough assessment. Timelines usually range from 2 to 6 weeks. Our strong lender relationships and streamlined processes help minimise delays.
