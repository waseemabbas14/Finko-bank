Why Choose a Business Line of Credit

A line of credit is an elegant solution for businesses seeking fluid access to capital without the rigidity of traditional loans. Whether managing seasonal fluctuations, covering short-term funding gaps, or seizing new opportunities, it provides continuous financial agility—keeping your business in control.

Eligibility & Process

At Finco Capital, we take a tailored approach to every facility. Our team analyses your business structure, financial performance, and funding objectives before matching you with the most suitable lender from our panel of major banks and non-bank specialists.

Typical Lender Assessment Criteria
  • Business Financials: Review of bank statements, financial reports, and balance sheets.
  • Time in Business: Demonstrated trading history and operational stability.
  • Serviceability: Evidence of consistent cash flow capable of servicing the facility.
  • Credit Profile: Lenders accommodate a wide range of credit histories—from prime through to complex.

Our process ensures each recommendation is transparent, efficient, and aligned with your business's long-term goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any fees?
Associated fees may include application, account maintenance, or facility management costs. Finco Capital presents all possible charges from our lender partners upfront, ensuring full transparency before you proceed.
How long can I access the funds for?
Most lines of credit are ongoing and subject to annual review. Many lenders provide multi-year terms with renewal flexibility to ensure continuous access to funds as your business evolves.
What can I use it for?
A Business Line of Credit is ideal for managing working capital, purchasing inventory, covering operating expenses, or funding growth and expansion initiatives.
