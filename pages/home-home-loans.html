Common Questions

What determines my borrowing capacity?
Your capacity considers income, living expenses, existing debts, and LVR. We provide a thorough assessment to ensure affordability and financial stability.
Should I choose a fixed or variable interest rate?
Fixed rates (e.g., from 5.69% p.a. for 2 years with Westpac) offer repayment certainty, while variable rates (e.g., from 5.64% p.a. with Macquarie) provide flexibility including offset accounts and extra repayments. We help you weigh options tailored to current markets.
What is Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI) and when is it needed?
LMI applies when your deposit is less than 20% (LVR above 80%). It protects the lender, not you. We explore strategies to reduce or avoid LMI, including lender policies for professionals.
How long does the home loan process take?
Applications typically process within 1 to 2 weeks after document submission. Pre-approvals are faster and can provide a competitive advantage.
Can I access home equity for other purposes?
Yes. You can leverage built equity for renovations, investments, or debt consolidation. We help you understand and structure your loan accordingly.
