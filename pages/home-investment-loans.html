Common Questions

What interest rates can I expect?
Investment loan rates tend to be slightly higher than owner-occupier loans. Our panel offers competitive loan options. We compare multiple offers to ensure you secure the best rate aligned with your investment strategy.
How much can I borrow?
Borrowing capacity depends on your gross income minus expenses and financial commitments. Lenders generally assess 80% of the estimated rental income to determine your serviceability. We perform a comprehensive evaluation to clarify your investment budget.
What is the difference between investment loans and home loans?
Purpose: Investment loans finance rental properties.
Interest rates: Typically 0.25% to 0.75% higher than owner-occupier rates.
Tax benefits: Interest expenses are usually tax-deductible.
LVR: Maximums often lower—typically 80-90% compared to 95% for owner-occupiers.
Is Lenders Mortgage Insurance required?
Yes, if your deposit is less than 20% (LVR above 80%), you will usually require LMI. Premiums may be higher for investment loans. We identify lenders offering competitive LMI rates and explore strategies to reduce this cost where possible.
How long does the application take?
Thanks to our efficient processes, approvals for straightforward investment loans are often finalised within 2 to 4 weeks. We handle all lender communications to keep things smooth.
Can I use equity from my home to invest?
Absolutely. Leveraging equity from your existing residence is a powerful strategy to grow your portfolio. We assist in calculating usable equity and structuring your loan accordingly.
