How Home Equity Loans Work

A home equity loan enables you to borrow against the difference between your property's current market value and your outstanding mortgage balance. This value—your equity—can be accessed as a lump sum or a revolving line of credit, ideal for both planned expenses and contingency funding.

Rates and terms vary by lender and purpose but generally require serviceability assessments and may involve Lenders Mortgage Insurance if LVR exceeds 80%.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate available equity?
Equity is the market value of your home minus the mortgage balance. We provide tools and expert advice to precisely estimate your usable equity.
Can I access equity for investment property purchases?
Yes. Many clients refinance to harness equity for investment purposes, subject to lender serviceability and LVR limits.
What repayment options are available?
Loan structures include principal & interest or interest-only repayments, tailored to your cash flow needs.
