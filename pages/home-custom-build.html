Common Questions

How does financing a house and land package work?

Financing occurs in two stages: a land loan for purchasing the vacant block and a construction loan releasing funds progressively (e.g., slab down, frame up, lock-up) as building phases complete. We expertly coordinate this lifecycle for you.

What deposit is required?

Typical deposits range from 10-20% of the combined land and construction value, funded via savings, equity, or eligible grants. We clarify your deposit requirements and explore all options.

Can I obtain pre-approval?

Yes. Pre-approval for the entire package secures a clear budget and strengthens your negotiating position with developers and builders. Our process assesses your complete borrowing capacity upfront.

What distinguishes a house and land package from buying an existing home?

The main difference is financing structure and timeline—packages involve a two-part loan and longer build schedule, while existing homes require a single mortgage. We handle the complexity to ensure you're fully supported.

Does your lender panel offer specific construction loans?

Yes. Our panel includes lenders like Macquarie Bank and La Trobe Financial, offering interest-only repayments during construction (often 18-24 months), easing cash flow as your home takes shape.
