Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I borrow?
Most lenders assess borrowing capacity based on your income, expenses, and existing commitments. Typically, this ranges from 4-6 times your annual income, though this varies by lender and individual circumstances.
What documents do I need?
Lenders typically require payslips, tax returns, bank statements, and proof of identity. Self-employed applicants may need additional documentation. We'll guide you through the full requirements.
Can I get a home loan with bad credit?
Yes. While credit history matters, many lenders assess applications holistically. We work with lenders who consider your overall situation, including reasons for past issues.
