Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get a home loan if I've lived overseas for several years?
Yes. Eligibility hinges on demonstrating stable overseas income and solid financial standing. We assess your case to match you with the right lender.
What interest rates can I expect?
Rates vary by lender, LVR, and income stability. While some specialist loans differ, we aim to secure competitive rates tailored to your needs.
How does the application process work from overseas?
Our streamlined process requires documents such as passports, visa/residency proof, overseas employment contracts, and bank statements. We act as your single point of contact liaising with Australian lenders.
Can I use an expat loan to buy investment property?
Absolutely. Many expats build Australian property portfolios. We help navigate investment-specific lending criteria including rental yield assessments.
What happens to my loan upon returning to Australia?
Notify your lender about your change of circumstances. Transitioning to local income often improves borrowing capacity and allows refinancing to more favourable home loan products.
Do I need LMI for an expat loan?
LMI requirements mirror domestic loans, typically required if borrowing exceeds 80% of property value. Costs vary by lender; we help explore strategies to manage or minimise this expense.
