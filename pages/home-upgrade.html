Upgrading My Home

Plan your home upgrade by calculating how much extra you can borrow against your current property equity.

What is a home upgrade loan?

A home upgrade loan lets you borrow against the equity in your existing home to fund renovations, extensions or improvements. It's an efficient way to increase property value while keeping your existing home loan intact.

How it works

  • Assess your current property value and loan balance.
  • Calculate available equity (property value minus loan balance).
  • Determine how much you can borrow for your upgrade project.

Frequently Asked

What can I use an upgrade loan for?
You can use funds for renovations, extensions, landscaping, new appliances, or any home improvement that adds value to your property.
How much equity do I need?
Most lenders require at least 20% equity remaining in your home. The more equity available, the more you can typically borrow.
