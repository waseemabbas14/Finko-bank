Frequently asked question

At Finco Bank, we believe in keeping our customers informed and confident about their banking decisions. Here you’ll find answers to the most common questions about our services, loans, and financial solutions.

General

What are Finco Bank’s working hours? ▲
Finco Bank branches are open Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, and closed on weekends and public holidays.
How can I open an account with Finco Bank?▲
You can easily open an account online through the Finco Bank website or mobile app, or visit your nearest branch with valid ID proof.
What is Finco Bank?▲
Finco Bank is a modern financial institution offering reliable banking, savings, and digital finance solutions for individuals and businesses.
What types of loans are available?▲
We offer personal loans, home loans, business loans, and auto financing with flexible repayment options.
How can I report suspicious activity on my account?▲
Immediately contact our 24/7 helpline or email the fraud department if you notice any unauthorized transaction.
